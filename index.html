Safety & responsibility

At IRIS, we prioritize safety and responsibility by rigorously testing and validating our AI systems, safeguarding user privacy, and ensuring transparency and explainability. We mitigate biases, provide human oversight, and employ robust security measures. Through collaboration with regulatory bodies, user empowerment through education, and responsible marketing practices, we promote ethical AI development and deployment, fostering trust and benefiting society.
